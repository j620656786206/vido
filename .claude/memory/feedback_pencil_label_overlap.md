---
name: Pencil Design Label Overlap
description: Design screen labels/titles must not overlap with other content in Pencil designs
type: feedback
---

When creating design screens in Pencil, ensure labels and titles do not overlap with other content elements.

**Why:** User reported difficulty reading designs when labels/titles visually collide with nearby content. This is about text overlapping within the same screen, not spacing between screens.

**How to apply:** When placing text labels or screen titles in Pencil batch_design operations, check coordinates to ensure adequate separation from surrounding elements. Verify with get_screenshot after placement.
