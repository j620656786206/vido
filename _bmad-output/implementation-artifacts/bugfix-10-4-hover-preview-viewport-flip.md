## Story

As a desktop user browsing a poster grid (homepage, library, search results, TMDb explore blocks),
I want hover feedback to appear **inside the card itself** — at the cursor's natural focal point — instead of as a separate panel that drops below the title/year row,
so that hovering feels immediate and discoverable, the action affordances (▶ play, ⋯ menu, select) are exactly where my eyes already are, and the experience matches the project's `.pen` design contract for `Component/PosterCardHover` (node `MQbvp`) instead of the unsanctioned floating-overview panel that crept in.
